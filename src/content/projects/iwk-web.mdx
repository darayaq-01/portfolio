## Requirements

Website developed for the Interkulturelle Wirtschaftskommunikation Institute from Friedrich-Schiller-Universität Jena. Style sober and clear for current and future students and university workers was required.

## Responsabilities

The site was developed in CMS Wordpress, with the purpose that it can be maintained and used autonomously by the staﬀ of the institute. A custom design was made based on a free template.

Also the Logo was required and done following the requirements of the client.

## Tech

- It used **WordPress** for the frontend and as a Content Management System (CMS), using a free template. I also take care of the security of the project as well as the SEO.
- **CSS3** to rich the very unique style required for the client.
- **PHP** to do some implementations were not possible directly with the template I used.
